Password Recovery

Forgotten passwords are among the most common causes of inaccessible crypto assets. Our specialists use advanced cryptographic techniques to recover or bypass passwords on all major wallet and exchange platforms.

Scenarios We Handle

  • Forgotten wallet encryption passwords and PINs
  • Lost seed phrases (partial or complete reconstruction)
  • Forgotten exchange account passwords with locked 2FA
  • Encrypted backup files (ZIP, 7z, PDF-protected wallets)
  • Legacy Bitcoin Core and Armory wallets with old encryption

Our Techniques

We employ GPU-accelerated password cracking, dictionary attacks with intelligent rule sets, and AI-assisted password reconstruction based on your memory hints. For seed phrases, we use combinatorial permutation algorithms to reconstruct partial phrases with missing or jumbled words.

Privacy Guarantee

All password recovery operations are performed locally in our secure facility. We never transmit your encrypted files over the internet. Your data stays completely private throughout the entire process.

Hardware Wallet Recovery

Devices We Work With

  • Ledger Nano S, X, and Stax (all models)
  • Trezor Model One and Model T
  • KeepKey, BitBox02, Coldcard, and Foundation Passport

  • Devices wiped without backup

Mobile Wallet Recovery

Smartphones hold enormous amounts of crypto wealth through mobile wallets. Lost phones, failed app updates, factory resets, or stolen devices can all lead to inaccessible funds — but CipherTrace Recovery has the tools to help.

What We Recover

  • Deleted wallet apps with remaining data in device storage
  • Wallets on lost, stolen, or broken smartphones
  • Recovery after factory reset or OS corruption
  • iCloud or Google Drive encrypted backups containing wallet data
  • PIN/biometric locked phones with wallet apps inside

iOS & Android Forensics

We use certified mobile forensics tools (Cellebrite, GrayKey, Oxygen Forensics) to perform deep extractions of app data, even after deletion. For iOS devices, we leverage iTunes backup decryption and file-system extractions. Android forensics include full-disk decryption and chip-off capabilities for non-functional devices.

Data Privacy

We extract only the cryptographic data needed for wallet recovery. Personal photos, messages, and other private data are never accessed, stored, or examined. A chain of custody document is provided for all mobile recovery cases.

What information do I need to provide?

How long does recovery take?

Simple cases (forgotten passwords with good information) can be resolved in 24–72 hours. Complex cases involving hardware damage or blockchain tracing typically take 5–14 business days.

We charge a success-based fee structure. After a free consultation and case assessment, we provide a fixed quote for the recovery service. Fees vary based on case complexity and asset amount.

Absolutely. All data is transmitted via 256-bit encrypted channels and stored in air-gapped secure servers. We never share client information with third parties and operate under strict data protection policies.

Can you help if funds were stolen very recently?

Yes — time is critical in theft cases. The faster you contact us, the higher the probability of tracing and recovering funds before they're laundered. Contact us via WhatsApp for immediate response in urgent cases.

Do you work with law enforcement?

Yes. We prepare forensic evidence packages suitable for submission to law enforcement agencies worldwide and have experience coordinating with cybercrime units in the US, UK, EU, and Australia.
